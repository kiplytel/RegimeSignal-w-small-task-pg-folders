How the RTS Score Works
RTS doesn't look at factor levels like MRS does. It measures bull recovery patterns and classifies current market regime across 5 validated stages. Entirely different math, entirely different purpose. Where MRS identifies bear-market deterioration up to 6 months early, RTS confirms bull recoveries an average of 1.8 months before the trough with 79% primary accuracy and 86% confirmation within 6 weeks.
FACTOR MOVEMENT, NOT FACTOR LEVELS
MRS asks "what are the current factor readings?" — RTS asks "how are those readings changing, diverging, and accelerating?" The RTS architecture focuses on factor movement, divergence, and breach velocity rather than static levels. This is what makes bull recovery detection possible: recoveries don't announce themselves with favorable absolute readings — they announce themselves with directional shifts in the underlying data.
5-STAGE REGIME CLASSIFICATION — 70% PRECISION
RTS classifies the current S&P 500 regime into one of five validated stages: Strong Bull, Moderate Bull, Marginal Bull, Neutral, and Bear. Each stage has been empirically validated across 432 months of market data with 70% full-precision accuracy. When collapsed to a 3-stage directional call (Bull / Neutral / Bear), accuracy reaches 81%.
